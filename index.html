Engineering and Technology for the Better Good of Society

Our Mission Statement


Train the incoming engineering technology student to leverage the power of select technologies aligned to the public interest applications and targeted public works institutions.

Offer pathways to public service careers by first making students aware that such options exist early in their studies with PIT-centric course work.
Module 1 Project Management Technology for various Public Works institutions
Components
Expose students to the importance of organized planning and creating dynamic scheduling offered only with professional software technology.


Deliverable
Step-by-step learning module on using MS ProjectTM.
The module includes transparent scheduling algorithm and user-friendly interface.


Lab Component
Three virtual case studies are created and stored as an OER resource.
The studies allow creation of virtual case study per instructor’s permission.
